World's Best Gins

Gin started out as a herbal remedy for stomach aches, gout and gallstones. It was claimed that the Dutch introduced juniper berries to flavour natural spirits to make it more palatable. Due to its subtle flavour, it is easy to mix and has become a staple alcohol of choice for a whole host of famous cocktails, including the all-time classic Martini.
